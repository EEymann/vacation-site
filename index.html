My dream vacation: The Fiji Islands

SUVA: the capital of the Fiji Islands

SUVA is the capital and largest metropolitan city in Fiji. It is located on the southeast coast of the island of Viti Levu, in the Rewa Province, Central Division.

SUVA is best known for:

  • It is one of happiest countries in the world!
  • With FIJI's lush scenic landscape, it is known for it's rainbow of colors.
  • You can't miss the striking waterfalls that cascade down the black lava rocks!
